# Product Context

## Problem Statement

Comic fans lack a unified, user-friendly platform for discovering and reading digital comics online.

## User Personas

- Casual Reader: Wants to browse and read comics without hassle.
- Collector: Wants to organize and bookmark a personal library.
- Admin: Needs to upload and manage comic content.

## User Experience Goals

- Fast, intuitive search and browsing
- Seamless reading experience
- Easy library management
- Secure and private user data
